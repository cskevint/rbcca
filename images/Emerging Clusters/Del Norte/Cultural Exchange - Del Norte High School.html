Cultural Exchange

A small boat belonging to Takata High School washed up on the beach of Crescent City in April, 2013.  Students successfully returned the boat to Rikuzentakata during September. Six students from Del Norte High School will visit Rikuzentakata and Takata High School from February 17th through the 19th, 2014.
